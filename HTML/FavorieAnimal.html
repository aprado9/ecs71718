The Madagascar Sunset Moth was found in 1773 by Dru Dury. At first he thought the insect was a butterfly but later in 1823, Jacob Hubner discovered that it was a moth and it was placed in the genus Chrysirida group. The moth is regarded as one of the most beautiful and looked for lepidopterans due to its colored wings and because of thistheir wings have been used for jewerly.However, despite the high demand for them they aren't endangered.
